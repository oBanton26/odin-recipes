Hot Chocolate

A good hot chocolate to give you some strength in a cold winter morning !

Description

The hot chocolate isn't a recipe that seems important but to me it is. So suck it.

My recipe is maybe the easiest one because you only need three basic ingredients (hehe it's the same sentence as for the crepes recipe)

Ingredients

  • Cacao powder
  • Sugar
  • Boiling water
  • Okay now you can had milk if you're a baby

Steps

  1. Boil your water
  2. Mix in your recipient the cacao powder and some sugar
  3. Add the boiling water to almost fill your mug
  4. Add some milk or cream to make it sweeter if you want (you baby...)
