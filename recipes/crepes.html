Description

The french crepes - or juste crepes, are the easiest and most delicious deserts or meals you can make. That's it really.

My recipe is maybe the easiest one because you only need three basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • I said 3 ingredients and I'm a man of my word so trust me.

Steps

  1. Add a pyramid of flour
  2. Add 2 or 3 eggs depending on the size of it
  3. Add water and mix to a texture that seems pleasing (oh how I hate recipes that leaves you hanging experienceless)
