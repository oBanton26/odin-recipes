Chocolate Cake

This is a chocolate cake duh...

Description

The chocolate cake is like the basic cake you need to learn how to cook.

The recipe itself isn't hard, but mastering it is a whole quest to perfection (it's my madeleine fo Proust).

Ingredients

  • Chocolate (of course)
  • Butter (the more the butter...)
  • Flour (I can get myself...)
  • Sugar (this demon)

Steps

  1. Heat the butter and the chocolate together
  2. Add the sugar and the flour next
  3. Mix everything
